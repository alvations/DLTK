DLTK: Deutsch Language Tool Kit

The Deutsch Language Tool Kit (DLTK) is an attempt to gather German Natural Language Processing (NLP) preprocessing tools under one free and open source python-able library. Usability and utility of DLTK remains the foremost importance in deriving best of practices for linguistically sound and computationally feasible solution for German NLP preprocessing.

Free and Open

We understand different ethos between the open and free software community but the issue with German NLP tools remain one of usability and utility. With regards to licenses, decision is yet to be made, but it will surely maintain the freedom of users and integrity of developers.

Usable and User-friendly

"Without users, developers built tools in vain. Without developers, users wait in vain for tools."
